Secure, Compliant, Efficient: Identity & Access Solutions by Nexus Group

Nexus Group is a leading provider of security solutions for physical and digital access. It include identity and access management (IAM) products, digital identities, and access solutions that ensure secure, compliant, and efficient operations for organizations of all sizes.

Secure Society By Enabling Trusted Identities

Empowering Secure and Efficient Access For Your Business

Nexus Group offers a comprehensive suite of identity and access management (IAM) solutions designed to streamline your control, strengthen security, and empower your workforce. Our products address the critical needs of modern businesses:

Nexus Smart ID

Nexus Smart ID is a comprehensive identity and access management platform that enables organizations to manage the entire lifecycle of digital identities. It ensures secure access to physical and digital resources.

Nexus Certificate Manager

Nexus Certificate Manager is a flexible and scalable platform for issuing and managing digital certificates. It supports multiple use cases, including user authentication, device authentication, and secure communication.

Nexus Personal Desktop Client

Nexus Personal Desktop Client is a software solution for secure and convenient user authentication and digital signing. It supports smart cards, tokens, and other secure devices.

Nexus Hybrid Access Gateway

Nexus Hybrid Access Gateway provides secure and unified access to both on-premises and cloud applications. It offers a range of authentication methods and integrates with various identity providers.

Top Notch Features

Nexus Group's Powerful Access Control Features

Multi-Factor Authentication (MFA)

Fortify access with multiple verification steps, including one-time passwords (OTP), biometrics, hardware tokens, and mobile authentication.

Single Sign-On (SSO)

Boost user experience with a single login for all applications. Centralized authentication and federated identity ensure seamless access and enhanced security.

Credential Management

Manage the entire lifecycle of digital and physical credentials, including secure issuance, automated renewals and revocations, secure storage, and a user-friendly self-service portal.

Role-Based Access Control (RBAC)

Simplify access control by assigning permissions based on user roles. Define roles, access policies, and leverage dynamic role assignment for effortless management.

Audit and Reporting

Maintain comprehensive oversight with detailed event logs, real-time security monitoring, customizable compliance reports, and anomaly detection for suspicious access patterns.

Mobile ID

Empower users with secure and convenient mobile identities. Leverage a user-friendly mobile app, secure enrollment processes, biometric authentication, and cross-platform compatibility.
